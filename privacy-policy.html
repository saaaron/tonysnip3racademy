Privacy Policy

Tonysniper Academy (“we,” “our,” or “us”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you access or use our website or services.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information from you:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, and other contact information you provide when registering for events, creating an account, or making payments.
  • Payment Information: When paying for courses or classes, we collect necessary billing and payment details (handled securely via third-party payment processors).
  • Usage Data: We may collect information about your device, browser, IP address, and pages visited on our platform.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide access to courses.
  • Manage event registrations and send confirmation or update emails.
  • Process monthly class payments securely.
  • Respond to inquiries, send updates, and improve our services.
  • Notify you of new events, updates, or promotions (you may opt out anytime).

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. However, we may share your data with:

  • rusted third-party service providers (e.g., payment processors, email automation tools) only to the extent necessary for service delivery.
  • Legal authorities if required to comply with applicable laws or regulations.

4. Data Secruity

We take reasonable steps to protect your personal data. However, no system is completely secure. We encourage you to use strong passwords and take care when sharing sensitive information.

5. Your Rights

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data by contacting us at support@tonysniperacademy.com

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The most recent version will always be posted on this page with the updated date.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the effective date at the top.

Your continued use of our services after any changes to this Privacy Policy constitutes your acceptance of such changes.
